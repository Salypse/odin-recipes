Caramel Apple Bars

Home

Description

These caramel apple bars are sure to become a fall favorite! This recipe works well with firm apples like Granny Smith or Honeycrisp.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/4 cups butter, melted
  • 2 cups apple, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups caramel topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 15x10 inch jellyroll pan
  2. To make the crust: Combine 2 cups of flour, quick cooking oats, brown sugar, and baking soda in a large bowl. Stir in butter and mix until crumbly
  3. Press half of the mixture into the prepared baking pan. Bake for 8 minutes
  4. To make caramel filling combine caramel topping and remaining 1/2 cup of flour in a small pan over medium heat. Bring to a boil and stir consistently for 3 to 5 minutes
  5. Spread apples evenly over the baked crust.Pour caramel filling over the apple layer. Add remaining crust over the top of everything. Bake for 20-25 minutes
  6. Let cool before serving
